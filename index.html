The Methodology
Five independent signals. Composite scoring.
01
Price Drawdown
Distance from 52-week high. Measures the magnitude of market rejection from peak price levels.
Weight: 20%
02
Put / Call Ratio
Options market positioning. Elevated put demand signals institutional hedging and defensive fear.
Weight: 20%
03
News Sentiment
Volume and tone of sector coverage over 30 days. Narrative fear often precedes or amplifies price dislocations.
Weight: 20%
04
Insider Buying
Smart money activity during drawdowns. The single strongest contrarian signal when insiders accumulate during public panic.
Weight: 25%
05
Earnings Revision
Analyst estimate momentum over 60 days. Separates temporary fear from genuinely deteriorating fundamentals.
Weight: 15%
